The life of a token

To capture the essence of Life, we will now follow the life of a token. I want you to imagine a small bit. Actually, not just a bit, but bits of ones and zeros. Like 0111000 or 1100110. And that‚Äôs good - said the Universe.

Well, that‚Äôs a great question! The secret to how these layers interact is emergence and feedback. What happens at one layer can give rise to new patterns, rules, and agents at the next.
